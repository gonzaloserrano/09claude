## Topic

A social example of using a background Codex workflow plus personal photos to generate a long-form children's book artifact.

## Research Summary

- The post itself is anecdotal, but the official OpenAI materials support the underlying capabilities it points to.
- The OCR from the images shows a multimodal prompt referencing photos and a previous book pass, alongside a background task result.
- Taken together, this suggests the workflow is no longer hypothetical: background execution, cloud delegation, and image-aware prompting can support this kind of artifact generation.

## Key Findings

- The images in the post describe creating a children's book about the past six months of the author's kids' lives, seeded with photos.
- OpenAI's Codex docs support background tasks, cloud-based delegation, CLI workflows, and image-aware prompting.
- The strongest claim here is practical feasibility, not reliability or output quality across all creative projects.
