## Topic
Interactive AI codebase tours for feature-oriented onboarding and maintenance

## Research Summary
This bookmark points to interactive ai codebase tours for feature-oriented onboarding and maintenance. External sources indicate this area is moving from demos toward repeatable workflow patterns, with clear productivity upside when paired with guardrails.

## Key Findings
- Feature-centric walkthroughs reduce cognitive load compared with file-by-file exploration.
- Interactive traces can help newcomers understand cross-cutting dependencies faster.
- Quality depends on indexing depth and how well the tool explains uncertain links.
