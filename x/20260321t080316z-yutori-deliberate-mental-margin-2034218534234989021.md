## Topic
Yutori: creating deliberate mental margin to reduce burnout

## Research Summary
The post describes “yutori” as intentionally slowing down to stay present. While often popularized online, the core idea—preserving cognitive margin—maps well to burnout-prevention and attention management practices.

## Key Findings
- “Yutori” is commonly explained as room/margin (time and mental space).
- Deliberate slack can improve decision quality and emotional regulation under constant context switching.
- Practical benefit comes from scheduling margin, not just endorsing the concept.
